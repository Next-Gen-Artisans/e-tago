Why Choose E-Tago?

Privacy is paramount in this day and age where the digital footprint of every individual is vast, particularly in e-commerce sites.

Product reviews are crucial in e-commerce and buyers desire to complete these reviews whenever they purchase a product due to rewards or incentives.

However, by completing these reviews without regard on exposed personal information, privacy may be compromised. E-Tago rises to this challenge.

By integrating computer vision techniques and deep learning algorithms, E-Tago offers a robust solution to detect and censor personal information within e-commerce product review images.

E-Tago is an Android-based mobile application for privacy preservation and image censorship of personal information in e-commerce product review images. This application will serve as a tool to safeguard personal information that might be shared on e-commerce platforms, in particular, product review images. E-Tago employs state-of-the-art computer vision algorithms, including YOLOv8 and Faster R-CNN, to detect personal information within product review images. Once detected, the app offers automatic censorship options to protect users' privacy.

E-Tago is capable of detecting and censoring a wide range of personal information, including names, addresses, phone numbers, tracking numbers, faces, QR codes, and barcodes that could potentially expose personal information in product review images.
